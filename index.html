About Me

I’m a graphic designer with a growing interest in web design and user experience. I enjoy creating clean designs and learning how to turn them into functional websites. Right now, I’m focused on continuing to build my skills and getting more comfortable combining design with how things actually work.

I’m also interested in continuing to grow as a designer and expanding my understanding of front end development. I want to build projects that not only look good but are easy to use and make sense for the user. As I gain more experience, I’m hoping to create work that reflects both strong design and practical functionality.

Hobbies

Graphic Design

I work on creating layouts, digital graphics, and branding materials for a range of projects. I enjoy balancing clean design with real-world use, making sure everything not only looks good but also communicates clearly and works across different formats.

Gaming

I enjoy playing Nintendo games, especially Animal Crossing, Mario, and Pokémon. I like the mix of creativity, exploration, and strategy, whether it’s building and customizing in Animal Crossing or working through challenges and leveling up in Pokémon.

Walks & Runs

I like going on walks or runs while listening to music. It’s a good way for me to clear my head, relieve stress, and get some energy out while still enjoying time outside. It also helps me reset, feel more focused, and end the day on a better note, especially after a long or busy day.

Goals

I’m working on improving my skills in both design and development, and I want to keep building projects that feel clean, creative, and easy to use.

  1. Get more comfortable with coding and front end development
